Recipe: Chicken biryani
Descirption: Biryani is a mixed rice dish that is thought to have originated from ancient Iran. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cate r to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat. 

Difficulty: Medium
Time: 1 h 0 m
Ingredients:yogurt 3tsp, onion 2pcs, ghee 1tsp, Chicken 500gm, green chilly 4, ginger paste 1tsp, salt
Steps: 1.firstly soak saffron in water to prepare saffron water
2.Next, mix kewra drops in water and mix well to make kewra water. Set them aside for later usage. Now, chop the onion and coriander leaves and keep them aside.
3.Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a deep bottomed pan. Once the oil is hot enough, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, green cardamom, black cardamom, cloves in it, and saute for about a minute
4.Then, add chopped onion to it and saute until pink. Now, add chicken into it with slit green chillies, turmeric, salt to taste, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder and green chilli paste. 
5.Mix well all the spices and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then, add hung curd into it and give a mix
6.Turn the flame to medium again and add garam masala in it along with ginger julienned, coriander and mint leaves.
7.Then add 1 cup cooked rice and spread evenly. Then add the remaining saffron water and pour ghee over it. 
8.Cook for 15-20 minutes with a closed lid and garnish with 1 tbsp fried onions and coriander leaves. Serve hot chicken biryani with raita of your choice. Enjoy!
